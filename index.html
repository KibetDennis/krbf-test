About Roll Ball

Briefly,

Roll ball is a popular sport, originated in the Asian region. It's a combination of Basketball,
Handball, Throwball and roller sports in which; two teams compete with each other. Roll ball can be played indoors as well as outdoors on hard surfaces such as wooden or asphalt. The Roll Ball World Cup is the highest official competition for Roll Ball, held biennially.
Today, the popularity of roll ball has reached to a greater extent as it is played in more than 40 countries worldwide.

Objective

The prime aim of the teams is to score maximum points than the opponent. The players can score
points by striking the ball effectively in the opponent's goalpost with fair skills and fast speed. The primary concern during the match is to continuously bounce the ball on the ground, even during a pass.

Pillars of Roll Ball

Roll Ball game stands on 4 pillars i.e Balance, Speed, Accuracy & Team-work which is very important in athletes life.
Everyone would understand it's importance and execute it in day to day life.

Speed is a vital component of this sport, as it enables players to create scoring opportunities and chase down opponents.
